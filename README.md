# Hi, I'm Shilpi 👋

I am a solar physicist studying solar eruptions through multiwavelength observations from space-based missions and ground-based radio observatories worldwide.

My research focuses on understanding the physical processes behind solar flares, coronal mass ejections (CMEs), and solar energetic events. I work with observational data across different wavelengths and enjoy collaborating with researchers to develop software tools that transform raw instrument data into calibrated science-ready datasets and enable advanced scientific analysis.
