# Hi, I'm Shilpi 👋

I am a solar physicist studying solar eruptions through multiwavelength observations from space-based missions and ground-based radio observatories worldwide.

My research focuses on understanding the physical processes behind solar flares, coronal mass ejections (CMEs), and solar energetic events. I work with multiwavelength observational data and enjoy collaborating with researchers to develop software tools that transform raw instrument data into calibrated science-ready datasets for advanced scientific analysis.

## ☀️ Research Interests

- Solar eruptions and transient solar phenomena
- Solar radio bursts and plasma processes
- Radio, X-ray, and EUV diagnostics of solar activity
- Scientific software development
- Open-source tools for astronomy and space science
